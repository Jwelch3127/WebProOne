Case Study Project Instructions

Steps and Requirements:

1.) The first step is to select a painter, sculpter or mixed media artist from anywher within the last two centuries ( between 1800 to the present day). You may NOT select any of the following artists: Van Gogh, Da Vinci, Michelangelo, Dali, Picasso, or Andy Warhol. The purpose is to be creative and hone your research skills, so it is required that you select an artist we may not have heard of, or do not know as much about.

2.)You may use any resources available to you to thoroughly research your selected artists life and work. I will also require that you properly cite your sources in MLA format. There is no limit to the amount of sources however, you need to have at least 3. Once you have gathered the information you will then write a short essay between 1-2 pages outlining the life, history, work and influence of the artist. Be sure to include in your essay the following information: name(s) (if the artist had a allias or other name), birthdate and date of passing (if applicable), years active, type of artistic movement they belong to (surrealism, impressionism, realism, abstaract, ect.), the themes found in their work, and what historical events at that time may have contributed to their work and or how society might have been influenced by their work.

3.) Lastly, you will assemble a short 5-10 minute presentation on your artist based on your essay to present to the class.
