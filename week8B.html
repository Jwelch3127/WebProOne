Group Project Instructions

Steps and Requirements:

1.) Each of you will be split into a group of four based on the five major artistic movements below. You may sign up for any of the five movements listed, however, once the group is full, you will be promted to select another.

2.) Once all groups are decided, you and your partners will research your chosen artistic movement. You may use any resources available to your to complete your research. You will then put together a 20-25 minute presentation that you and your group will then deliver to your peers.

3.) The presentations must be dynamic and utilizing at least one other medium other than PowerPoint. Alternative media can include: video, audio, handouts, etc. Finally the presentation must include a type of activity to engage the class.

4.) Lastly, the presentation must respond to the following prompts: Who were the major contributors of these movements, where did they start, was there anything significant about the historical context that may have lead to the creation or popularization of the movement, what do you beleive might have happened if the movment never existed.
