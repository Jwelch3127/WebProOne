What is Stress Management?

Welcome to the wonderful world of stress management. Stress Management is the practice of using strategies to reduce the negative effects of stress on the mind and body. It involves techniques such as deep breathing, meditation, exercise, time management, and social support.Here you will begin your knowledge journey of what these strategies are, and take some time to reflect on your own stress. Feel free to explore and crush stress today!
